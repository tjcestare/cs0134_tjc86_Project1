  • How many people will be speaking?

  • When will I get my ticket after purchasing it?

    • We encourage you to use public transportation in order to get to the event. There will be limited parking spaces because the event is being held downtown.

    • We do cap the number of tickets one can purchase at 5. If you need more tickets than this, please contact us.
